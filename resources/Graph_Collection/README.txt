For these graphs different data sources with different licences were used.

1. Airport points:
	- This data set is in the public domain. 
	- The data can be found under https://hub.arcgis.com/datasets/esri-de-content::world-airports/about
	
2. POI Germany points:
	- The data used is provided by the OpenStreetMap Foundation(OSMF): https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Main_Page
	- The data is available under the Open Database License: http://opendatacommons.org/licenses/odbl/1.0/. 
	Any rights in individual contents of the database are licensed under the Database Contents License: http://opendatacommons.org/licenses/dbcl/1.0/
	- Credit is due to the ©OpenStreetMap contributors
	
3. Berlin streets:
	- The provider of the data is Geoportal Berlin / Lichtsignalanlagen. The data is licensed under Data licence Germany – attribution – Version 2.0; 
	The license text is available at www.govdata.de/dl-de/by-2-0. The data is located under https://hub.arcgis.com/maps/esri-de-content::stra%C3%9Fennetz-berlin/about. 
	
4. Toulouse points:
	- The data used is provided by the OpenStreetMap Foundation(OSMF): https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Main_Page
	- The data is available under the Open Database License: http://opendatacommons.org/licenses/odbl/1.0/. 
	Any rights in individual contents of the database are licensed under the Database Contents License: http://opendatacommons.org/licenses/dbcl/1.0/
	- Credit is due to the ©OpenStreetMap contributors
	
5. Alaska airports:
	- The data is downloadable under https://qgis.org/downloads/data/
	- The dataset used is the VMAP0 dataset, which has been converted from VPF format to SHP format and kindly provided by Andras Fabian. 
	Then the data were adapted for alaska dataset. For further information about the original dataset, please refer to http://gis-lab.info/qa/vmap0-eng.html 
	
6. Alaska elevation raster data:
	- The data is downloadable under https://qgis.org/downloads/data/
	- Dataset: SRTM - Alaska SRTM elevation model
	- Information on SRTM data (Shuttle Radar Topography Mission) are available at: http://srtm.csi.cgiar.org/ 

7. Alaska land cover raster data:
	- The data is downloadable under https://qgis.org/downloads/data/
	- Dataset: AVHRR Global Land Cover Classification (ERDAS IMG)
	- Information on AVHRR data (Global Land Cover Classification) are available at: https://geog.umd.edu/feature/global-land-cover-facility-%28glcf%29
	- The data are classified and colored according to information from glcf. Both color and reclass tables are provided with the data.
